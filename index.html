About me

What do I do?

I'm a skilled Performing Artist with a wealth of diverse life and technical experience that I bring to every project. I perform as Pure Akan, rapping in elective Twi and ocassional two words of English and some Ghanaian languages.

How do I do it?

I follow form as well as function. I know that the aesthetics of effectitive musical composition combined with application of a blend of old and new Ghanaian trends od musical flavour creates a unique style for my music and sets me apart from the others.

Why hire me?

I work tirelessly and effectively in getting tracks laid. I rehearse and listen to tracks constantly to ensure synchrony with all instruments. I engage well with my audience on all media and thrive among like minded people. I am a solo artist but have worked with many reputable artists too.
